About the Event

The Department of Chemical Engineering, IIT Jodhpur, proudly presents the third edition of Chem-E-Sorption 2026. This premier technical event features innovative competitions, expert lectures, and a dynamic environment for learning and collaboration.

Designed for aspiring chemical engineers and research scholars, it bridges the gap between theory and practice, offering participants the opportunity to tackle real-world challenges.
